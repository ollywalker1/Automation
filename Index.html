Strategy Explanation: Intraday Scalping Strategy

Core Philosophy

This strategy is designed for **scalping**—capitalizing on short-term intraday volatility for small, quick gains (typically 1-3%). Success relies on finding **confluence**, where multiple technical indicators across different low timeframes align to signal a high-probability entry.

Phase 1: Finding Potential Setups (Every 15 Minutes)

  • Broad Market Scan: The system scans the top 1000 Binance altcoins by volume.
  • Setup Filtering (15-Minute Chart): It filters this list to find coins showing signs of a potential reversal or continuation, looking for two key conditions:
    1. The price is currently near, or has just bounced off, the **55 EMA**.
    2. A classic **bullish RSI divergence** is present (price makes a lower low while RSI makes a higher low).
  • Create Watchlist: All coins that pass this setup filter are added to a "watchlist" for constant, real-time monitoring.

Phase 2: Identifying the Buy Trigger (Continuous)

  • Real-time Monitoring: The system scans every coin on the watchlist, every minute, looking for a final two-part buy trigger on lower timeframes:
    1. 5-Minute Chart: The **MACD histogram** must show increasing momentum (a "tick up").
    2. 3-Minute Chart: The most recent candle must be a strong **bullish confirmation pattern** (e.g., Hammer, Bullish Engulfing).
  • Execute Trade: When a coin on the watchlist meets these final trigger conditions, a buy signal is immediately sent. The system only allows one trade to be active at a time.

Phase 3: Managing and Exiting the Trade (Real-Time)

  • Activate Sell Monitoring: Once a trade is active, the system monitors it every 30 seconds for an exit signal.
  • Send Sell Webhook: A "close" webhook is sent as soon as **one** of the following exit conditions is met (whichever comes first):
    1. A **Bearish RSI Divergence** is detected on the 15-minute chart.
    2. The **MACD histogram** begins to downtick on the 5-minute chart, signaling lost momentum.
    3. The price closes **below the 55 EMA** on the 15-minute chart, invalidating the trend.
  • Log and Reset: The trade is logged, and the system returns to scanning for the next high-probability setup.
