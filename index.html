Strategy Explanation: The Triple Confluence Scalp

Core Concept & Multi-Timeframe Logic

This strategy identifies high-probability scalp trades by aligning trend, momentum, and volume across three distinct timeframes.

  • Higher Timeframe (1-Hour): Establishes the dominant trend using the 50 EMA and identifies major Support/Resistance levels.
  • Mid-Timeframe (15-Minute): Confirms setup and momentum alignment with the 1-hour trend using RSI and MACD.
  • Execution Timeframe (5-Minute): Provides the final trigger for entry when all conditions converge.

Step-by-Step Execution Plan (Automated)

The scanner automatically performs the following checks to identify opportunities:

  • Trend Direction (1H): Price must be above the 50 EMA for a bullish bias (or below for bearish).
  • Momentum Confirmation (15M): For a bullish setup, RSI(21) must be above 50.
  • Entry Timing (15M): A bullish MACD(12,26,9) crossover must occur (MACD line > Signal line).
  • Volume Validation (15M): The volume of the crossover candle must be greater than the previous candle's volume.
  • Final Trigger (5M): The tool continuously scans qualifying coins. A webhook is sent when a bullish MACD crossover with increasing volume also occurs on the most recently closed 5-minute candle, confirming the entry.
